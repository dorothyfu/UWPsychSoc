We are an official student-run organization that exists to support and benefit psychology undergraduate students both academically and socially. Basically, that means we run cool events for Psych undergrads, like destressers, guest lecturers, BBQs, and various activities! We’re entirely volunteer run and are funded by the ASU and the Society Fund.
Come hang out in our awesome office in PAS 3080 - any psychology undergrad is welcome to come to the office anytime we’re open. We have a kettle, a fridge, a microwave and super comfy couches, plus a really cheap tuck shop that sells chocolate, pop, juice, chips and noodle bowls.
Want to join? Good news, if you’re a Psych undergrad, you’re already a member! That means you can take part in any event, come voice your opinion and suggestions at meetings and hang out in our office!
